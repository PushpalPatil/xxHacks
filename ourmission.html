Girl Helpline is a community created to support high school girls who have experienced any kind of harrassment. Everyday, hundreds of girls face sexual harassment and sexual assault in their school environment.

They may be too scared to tell their friends, families, or teachers, and as a result, never receive the emotional help they deserve.

This could lead to a downward spiral of depression and self-blame.

Here on Girl Helpline, girls who have faced any type of harassment can ask for help anonymously and help others girls from their school.
